About Team Torque and Triumph

We are a passionate and innovative team dedicated to designing and building a high-performance kart. As a dynamic group of creative, hardworking and goal-oriented individuals, we bring together diverse skills in engineering, design and problem solving. With a competitive spirit and hands-on approach, we aim to push the boundaries and create a kart that reflects our bold, ambitious and future-focused mindset.

About CIT Motorsports

Coimbatore Institute of Technology Motorsports

CIT Motorsports Team was started in 2013. Over the past 10+ years, our college motorsports team has participated in various events across India and won awards across different categories, showcasing innovations, teamwork, dedication, and engineering excellence in every competition.
